A national mission for those who served

They stood the line. Now we stand with them.

A nationwide assistance program for veterans and first responders. Wherever you are, whatever you’re up against — reach out, and we get you the help you need.

I Need HelpSupport the Mission

No matter where you live, you never have to face it alone.

How It Works

Three steps. One promise.

Getting help should be simpler than the fight that got you here. It is.

01

Reach Out

One call, one message — from anywhere. Tell us what you’re facing. No wrong door, no red tape.

02

We Assess

We listen, verify the need, and find the fastest real path to help.

03

We Act

We fund the help you need, or reach out on your behalf to trusted local professionals.

What We Do

Help with whatever you’re carrying

We either fund the help directly or connect you with vetted local professionals — the right contractor, doctor, counselor, or specialist.

Our Mission

Built by one of their own

Dog Tags for Life exists for a simple reason: too many who served come home and hit a wall alone — a repair they can’t afford, a doctor they can’t reach, a loss they can’t face. We are the number they can call when there’s nowhere else to turn.

We’re a national nonprofit, so it doesn’t matter what state you’re in or what you need. You reach out once, and we go to work.

SGM Jim Taylor, U.S. Army (Ret.)Founder · Michigan National Guard
